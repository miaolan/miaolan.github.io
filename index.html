I am a PhD student in Operations Research at Cornell University . My current research focuses on the design and analysis of online algorithms for problems arising in operations research and computer science. I was awarded an NSERC fellowship in 2019 supporting this research.

I received my undergraduate degree from the University of Waterloo in 2018, majoring in Computer Science and Combinatorics/Optimization .

Previously, I have been a quantitative trading intern at Jane Street Capital, and a software engineering intern at Wish .
